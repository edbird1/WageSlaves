About Wage Slaves

We're a group of friends who love to game together. We play on all platforms, but we prefer PC.

We play a large variety of games. We play some A LOT more than others. Our favorites include: Minecraft, Counter-Strike: Global Offensive, Borderlands 2, Left 4 Dead 1/2, and Tera.

Want to join?

We have a few requirements to join the group:

  • You must have a sense of humor. We like to joke around a lot.
  • You must join our Line chat or a voice chat, such as Skype or Discord.
  • You must be mature (age doesn't define maturity; rational thinking does).
  • You must know how to have fun! (That is why we all play games, right?)

If you're interested in joining the group, the easiest way of contacting us is through Steam. Send a message to Bat, Fade, Justin, Chris or Fish.
